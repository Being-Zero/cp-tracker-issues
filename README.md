# CPTracker Issues
Exclusively for reporting issues or requesting/suggesting features for CPTracker (cpt.mentorpick.com).

Before reporting any issue with regards to your data, please read the following to understand how CPTracker collects and generates the leaderboard.

1. Check you have given correct usernames and you are solving problems only in given accounts.
2. Data from different sites is CRAWLED once in every 24 hours AUTOMATICALLY, it's not real-time.
3. Once your data is crawled, it takes 60 minutes time for leaderboard refresh.
4. You can request to update your own score once in 24 hours.
5. Source of your data for CPTRACKER is your "public profile page" on CODECHEF/CODEFORCES/LEETCODE.  if your data on your profile page in respective site, is incorrect, contact respective site's support team. Before reporting an issue related to data update, please make sure the information is updated and available on the respective website.
6. For Codeforces, you may notice that the solved problem count in CPTracker may not match the count listed on Codeforces profile page. This is because, the solved problem count is computed by adding up unique accepted problems in the submissions tab of the profile page. This is more accurate representation of solved count, compared to the count mentioned on the profile page.
7. After contest ends, solved problems aren't considered as CONTEST SOLVED COUNT.
8. Only contests where submissions were made while the contest is running are considered PARTICIPATED.
9. "My Contests" in Mentorpick.com does not mean participation. It only means you have access to those contests.

While reporting issue, please add screen shots and provide relevant information to help the team investigate.
